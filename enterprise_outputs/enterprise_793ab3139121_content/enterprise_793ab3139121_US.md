Enterprise AI refers to the application of artificial intelligence technologies, such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, to drive business value and improve operational efficiency. 1. **Cloud-based AI**: Cloud computing has become the preferred platform for AI deployment, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness.
2. **Explainable AI**: As AI becomes more pervasive, the need for explainability and transparency is growing, driving the development of techniques to interpret and understand AI decision-making processes.
