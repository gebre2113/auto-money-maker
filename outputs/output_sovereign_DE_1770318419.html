cases. 3. **Overfitting and Underfitting**: Overfitting occurs when the model is too complex and fits the training data too closely, while underfitting occurs when the model is too simple and fails to capture the underlying patterns in the data. 4. **Class Imbalance**: Class imbalance occurs when one class has a significantly larger number of instances than the others, which can affect the model's performance. ### 1.2 Advanced Troubleshooting Techniques Here are some advanced troubleshooting techniques that you can use to diagnose and fix issues related to DE models: 1. **Visualize the Loss Function**: Visualizing the loss function can help you understand how the model is performing and identify potential issues. 2. **Use Debugging Tools**: Debugging tools such as TensorBoard and PyTorch's built-in debugger can help you identify and fix issues related to the model and its training process. 3. **Monitor the Model's Performance**: Monitoring the model's performance on a
