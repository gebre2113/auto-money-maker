Cloud Setup

Our cloud setup will be designed to provide a flexible, scalable, and secure platform for our digital applications and services.   • Software as a Service (SaaS): We will use SaaS to provide software applications over the internet, eliminating the need for local installation and maintenance.
  • Cloud Management Platform: We will use a cloud management platform to manage and monitor our cloud infrastructure, applications, and services. This platform will provide features such as resource provisioning, workload management, and cost optimization.

We will also use data deduplication, compression, and encryption technologies to reduce storage costs and improve data security.

  • Power and Cooling: We will ensure that our data centers and other facilities are equipped with efficient power and cooling systems to minimize energy consumption and reduce our carbon footprint.
