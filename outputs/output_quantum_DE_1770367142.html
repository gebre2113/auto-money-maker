Technical FAQ

Below are 40 technical FAQ questions with detailed answers to help you deepen your understanding of the subject.

  1. Q: What is the difference between a hub and a switch?

    A: A hub is a simple network device that connects multiple devices together, while a switch is a more intelligent device that can learn the MAC addresses of connected devices and forward data packets to the intended recipient.

  2. Q: How does a router work?

    A: A router works by connecting multiple networks together and routing traffic between them. It uses a combination of IP addresses, subnet masks, and routing tables to determine the best path for data packets to travel.

  3. Q: What is the purpose of a firewall?

    A: The primary purpose of a firewall is to control incoming and outgoing network traffic based on predetermined security rules. It helps to prevent unauthorized access to a network and protect against malicious attacks.

  4. Q: How does a VPN work?

    A: A VPN (Virtual Private Network) works by creating a secure, encrypted tunnel between two endpoints. It allows users to access a private network over the internet, while maintaining the security and integrity of the data being transmitted.

  5. Q: What is the difference between a LAN and a WAN?

    A: A LAN (Local Area Network) is a network that spans a small geographic area, such as a home or office building, while a WAN (Wide Area Network) is a network that covers a larger geographic area, such as a city or country.

  6. Q: How does a Wi-Fi network work?
