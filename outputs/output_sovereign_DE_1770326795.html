For example, let's assume an investment of 100 DE coins generates a revenue of 150 DE coins over a 3-month period. The ROI would be: ROI = (Revenue / Initial Investment) x 100 = (150 DE coins / 100 DE coins) x 100 = 150% **ROI IN USD** ROI in USD is calculated by converting the revenue and initial investment from DE currency to USD and then performing the ROI calculation. For example, let's assume an investment of 100 DE coins is equivalent to $10,000 in USD and generates a revenue of 150 DE coins equivalent to $30,000 in USD over a 3-month period. The ROI would be: ROI = (Revenue / Initial Investment) x 100 = ($30,000 / $10,000) x 100 = 300% **COMPARING ROI IN DE CURRENCY VS.
