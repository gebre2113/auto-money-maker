Infrastructure Setup

Our infrastructure setup will be designed to be highly available, scalable, and secure.   • Virtual Private Cloud (VPC): We will create a VPC to provide a secure and isolated environment for our infrastructure.
  • Subnets: We will create multiple subnets to provide a high level of availability and scalability for our infrastructure.
  • Load Balancers: We will use load balancers to distribute traffic across multiple instances, providing a high level of availability and scalability.
  • Auto Scaling: We will use auto-scaling to dynamically adjust the number of instances based on traffic demand, providing a high level of scalability and cost-effectiveness.

Local Resource Scaling

Local resource scaling is critical to ensuring that our digital platform can meet the increasing demands of the local market. We will implement the following strategies to scale our local resources:

  • Content Delivery Network (CDN): We will use a CDN to cache and distribute content across multiple locations, reducing latency and improving user experience.

  • Local Data Centers: We will establish local data centers to provide a high level of availability and scalability for our infrastructure, while also reducing latency and improving user experience.
